Welcome to Bobwhite Foods

Bobwhite Foods is one of the largest importers of premium quality raw peanuts, offering a wide variety including red kernels, blanched peanuts, medium and jumbo sizes, as well as peanut splits.

We specialize in both small and bulk orders — from 50kg to 20 tons — and provide reliable shipment services worldwide. With decades of expertise, Bobwhite Foods has become a trusted partner for food manufacturers, wholesalers, and retailers across the globe.

Why Choose Us?

  • Wide range of raw peanut varieties
  • Flexible order sizes (50kg to 20 tons)
  • Worldwide shipping services
  • Trusted by global clients for quality & reliability
