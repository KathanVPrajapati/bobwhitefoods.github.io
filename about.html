About Us

At Bobwhite Foods, we are passionate about delivering the finest peanuts from trusted sources. As one of the biggest importers in the industry, we pride ourselves on our ability to provide high-quality peanuts to businesses of all sizes.

Our portfolio includes red kernels, blanched peanuts, medium & jumbo varieties, and splits, ensuring we can meet the diverse needs of our clients.

With a strong logistics network, we offer seamless order fulfillment from 50kg bags up to 20-ton shipments. Our commitment to quality and customer service has earned us long-standing relationships with food manufacturers, distributors, and retailers around the world.

At Bobwhite Foods, your success is our priority — we don’t just sell peanuts, we build partnerships.
